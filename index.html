THE MOROCCAN FASHION DEVELOPMENT™:

  • Who are M.F.D ?

    • M.F.D is a platform that develops the finished product for Men, Women, Children, intended for the services of Brands and Companies producing for National and International Fashion.

  • What service does M.F.D offer to its customers ?

    • With our design office equipped with high-performance equipment and a professional technical team of : Styling, Modeling, Merchandising, Technique in Method, Industrialization, Quality Control and CSR, Sourcing of Ecological, Organic, or Recycled Materials and Supplies, while meeting the requirements and standards International Standards, And in partnership with Moroccan clothing companies who are committed and ready to take up this challenge with us.

  • Why choose M.F.D as a partner ?

    • We are concerned about respecting the environment.
    • The sustainability of nature.
    • Contribution to human rights services are among our primary concerns.
    The thing that forces us to be very careful and require audits during our selection of our raw materials and our partners.
